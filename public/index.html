Privacy Policy

1 Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all members, residents, visitors, and users of the Society Management services and is to be displayed on the Society Management website.

2 Policy

2.1 Objectives

2.1.1 Society Management adheres to Federal, State, and Local regulatory and privacy requirements. This policy outlines the practices applicable to the management and protection of personal information.

2.1.2 We collect personally identifiable information only when it is voluntarily submitted by our members, residents, or visitors. The information provided is used to fulfill specific requests and improve our services, unless given permission to use it for other purposes.

2.1.3 In connection with the services we provide, Society Management may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, birthdates, and other contact information.
  • Financial Information: Payment details, bank account information, and transaction history.
  • Cookies: When you visit our website, cookies are used to recognize your browser and collect information about how you use our site. These cookies do not contain personal data.
  • Communications: Emails, phone calls, and other communications with Society Management may be retained to address inquiries, respond to requests, and improve our services.
  • Community Interaction: Information provided through community forums, events, and other social interactions within the society.

3 Use of Information

3.1 Society Management uses the collected information to:

  • Provide and manage services for society members and residents.
  • Process payments and manage financial transactions.
  • Communicate important information regarding society events, meetings, and updates.
  • Enhance user experience and improve the quality of our services.
  • Ensure the safety and security of the society's residents and property.

4 Sharing of Information

4.1 We do not share personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect and defend the rights and property of Society Management.
  • To service providers who assist us in our operations, under strict confidentiality agreements.

5 Your Rights

5.1 You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of receiving non-essential communications.
  • Restrict the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
